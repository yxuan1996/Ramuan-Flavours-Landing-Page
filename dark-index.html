Who are we?

Ramuan Flavours is a young, dynamic and innovative manufacturer of quality desserts and cakes. We are skilled for creating recipes that bakes into attractive, mouth-watering pastries with diverse inventory of products. We focused on latest techniques of naturally balance ingredients, resulting in excellent flavor with professional qualities that any chefs expect. We are proud to maintain the fine reputation of homemade tastes and creating first-rate, top quality pastry products. From our frozen dough to our basic baking ingridients, we seek to satisfy our customer with quality service behind every single ingredients we offer. Our products are Halal certified by the Islamic Development Department of Malaysia (JAKIM), which is recognized worldwide. Our ultimate aim is to prepare ingredients that will please and assist you.
